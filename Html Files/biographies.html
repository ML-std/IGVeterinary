Dr.Ashley Zieglar

A bright and refreshing addition to the AVS team; meet Dr. Ashley Zieglar, DVM (Doctor of Veterinary Medicine) who is practicing veterinary medicinee at our Kingwood, WV and Uniontown, PA locations! Dr. Zieglar is a graduate of the University of Missouri College of Veterinary Medicine. She grew up in Greene County, Pennsylvania on a beef cattle farm. Dr. Zieglar holds a special interest in agriculture close to her heart and was very active in 4-H where she was inspired to start her very own sheep flock. In her free time outside the world of Veterinary Medicine, she continues to raise cattle and sheep with her family. Dr. Zieglar is adored by staff, clients, and patients. She is known for her caring, kind, and friendly demeanor and has a heart of gold. She truly values the care that she provides to each & every one of her patients. She is a crazy cat mom to a spoiled rotten calico named Fuzzy Butt. We are so happy to have Ashley as part of the AVS team and brand. click to mail Dr. Ashley!
