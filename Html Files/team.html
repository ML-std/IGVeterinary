Our Team

Our first AVS clinic was opened in Uniontown, PA on December 17th of 2014 by Founder, Dr. Ben Baker and Partner, Dr. Amy Nicewonger. From there, we started expanding into different areas of the Northeast region and met new Partners along the way! In 2017, we opened our doors at AVS Vet Express in Morgantown, WV where Dr. Robert Fraley joined us in Partnership. Then in 2018, we opened our third location AVS Mountainland Animal Hospital in Kingwood, WV. We take pride in providing quality health care to our patients while maintaining a comfortable and educational working environment for all employees.
